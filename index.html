j3nnalive

tiktok's least favourite musician

j3nnalive is a musician and gaming content creator hailing from West Yorkshire, specialising in alternative-rock and speedrunning video games. Her work explores issues of being transgender in a world where her identity is used as a political tool and scapegoat by those who want to put her down and eradicate her through her revolutionary lyrics, aggressive sound, and her monumental achievements in gaming. Amassing a combined following of 94,000 across her social media platforms, j3nna has performed her music across the country from her home town Bradford all the way to London and Brighton. Additionally, she has held numerous records across her career as a speedrunner including the world record for blinfolded Minecraft. Currently, j3nna is seeking to expand her career through extensive gigging, music releases, and gaining another few world records to add to her belt all in the name of silencing transphobes and uplifting the voices of her community.
